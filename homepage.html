12 Things NOT To Do In Italy

Here is a list of 12 things that you should totally avoid doing for actually exploring Italy like the Italians do! Make sure you take a note of each and every one of them :

  1. Expect A Lavish Spread
  2. Board Without Validating Your Ticket
  3. Order A Latte (Please Don’t)
  4. Wear A Tube Top Or Shorts To A Church
  5. Ask For Still, Thinking It’s Tap
  6. Believe Google Is Your Best Friend (Not Always)
  7. Take them Thinking They Are Free
  8. Schedule Sightseeing Tour On Sundays
  9. Carry Heavy Suitcases
  10. Rely Only On Your Credit Cards
  11. Not Eating Pizza With Your Hands
  12. - Dine Near Major Tourist Spots

Let's schedule your "Tour de Italia." Start in Rome, where gladiators once beat the shit! out of each other, in the Colosseum - don't worry, you're not expected to fight any lions like Maximus did. Then, go north to Florence for some VIP time with Botticelli, and don't forget to give the Leaning Tower of Pisa a friendly Push - In Venice, you'll feel like a character in storybook as you navigate canals and visit a palace that's fancier than your wildest dreams.
Milan's got a Leonardo masterpiece for you, and Naples will treat you with both ancient city vibes and a Pizza Napolitana - DO not Request for a change ... And that's just the beginning.
