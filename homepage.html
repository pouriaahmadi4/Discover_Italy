  2. Board Without Validating Your Ticket
  3. Order A Latte (Please Don’t)
  4. Wear A Tube Top Or Shorts To A Church
  5. Ask For Still, Thinking It’s Tap
  6. Believe Google Is Your Best Friend (Not Always)

  8. Schedule Sightseeing Tour On Sundays

The Story of 3 girl tourists who traveled to Italy to find and eat the best pizza in the world

The Pizza Seekers' Dilemma

Once upon a time in the land of Italy, there was Three clueless tourist named Dina, Mobina and Elia who had a singular mission: to find and eat the best pizza in the world. However, They were faced with a series of challenges:



The City Conundrum

Elia scratched her head, told the other two that she thinks its almost impossible to find the best Pizza between the ocean of pizza legends of Naples and the well-known pizza gems hidden in other cities. She wondered if they could satisfy their pizza cravings while going on a city-hopping adventure.



The Pizzeria Predicament

On their mission to find the best, they confronted with a sea of pizzerias. How could they distinguish the good from the great, the exceptional from the edible? They dreamt of a Pizza GPS that would guide them to the mighty Pizza directly.



The Questionable Adviser

Just as they were about to give up, a peculiar,handsome character named Pinocchio with a sharpy nose appeared in front of them. With a nose that seemed to grow with every word, Pinocchio claimed to know the secrets of pizza secrets. Was he a true mentor or was he saying nonesense, they asked themselves !!!



The Bizarre Encounters

Their journey took them through weird villages and enchanting landscapes. Along the way, they met a talking tomato with a face like Mona Lisa, a dancing pizza cutter resembling Al Pacino, and other weird looking things.they thought that must have has something to do with Pinocchio or even greater "this Country" .Each encounter left them more puzzled than the last.



The Mysterious Ingredients

Pinocchio insisted that the secret to the ultimate pizza is in the choice of ingredients. he told them they have to mix unusual toppings like gummy bears and pickles to create a pizza masterpiece. Could this weird combinations lead to perfection?
